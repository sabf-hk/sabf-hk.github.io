Purpose & Objectives

The purpose of the South Africa Business Forum is to provide a communication/ facilitation platform between South Africa, Hong Kong and China business executives:

  • Serve as a forum for networking
  • Provide business development opportunities
  • Facilitate for members to promote their products and services
  • Keep members informed of relevant business, trade and legal development in the respective territories
  • Be a source of information and assist individuals and companies on the lookout for business, trade or investment
  • Offer a complete directory of members/companies
  • Represent forum members on issues of common interest
  • Organize informative seminars, discussions, luncheons and social events
  • Establish and preserve good relationships with other business chambers
  • Encourage ethical and responsible business practices
  • Generally promote South Africa
  • Support the vision and objectives of the Southern African Development Community (SADC)
  • Work in partnership with representatives of the South African Consulate-General Hong Kong SAR / Macau SAR, PRC
  • Encourage participation in the Hong Kong Community
