The South African Business Forum strives to serve as a forum for South African businesses and organisations, encouraging and promoting interest in South Africa, exchanging views relating to business in Asia, and facilitating development regarding successful communication and interaction.

SABF allows development and promotion of sound relations between business, government and other relevant organisations in Hong Kong. The key focus of the forum is to utilise and share information relevant to trade, tourism, investment and alternative business opportunities available in Hong Kong and ensure their accessibility to the greater market.

Hong Kong is both an established business capital of the world and an international finance centre. This creates a positive message within the international business community as businesses from all continents are embraced by them.

The SABF recognises this and aims to bridge the gap between communication and facilitation of dynamic businesses and industries in both Hong Kong and Southern Africa.
