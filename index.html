Welcome to the South African Business forum of Hong Kong. The SABF serves as a medium aimed at increasing the profile of dynamic and influential South African business in Hong Kong, the facilitation of business development and networking opportunities between these two continents.

Peruse our site at your leisure and if you are interested in participating in the SABF please do not hesitate to contact us.
