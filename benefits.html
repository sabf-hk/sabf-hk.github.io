Benefits for SABF-HK Members:

South African Business Forum Members in Hong Kong are eligible for benefits, discounts and special offers from a variety of restaurants and service providers, these benefits are listed below, and are added to regularly:

Business

Lifestyle

OUT OF AFRICA RESTAURANT
Kuala Lampur, Malaysia

Out of Africa offers members of the SABF-HK a 15% discount across their full bill. This discount applies to full prices on the A La Carte menu for both food and beverages.
We regret that this discount is not apply to functions.

For further details or bookings please contact Dave Solomon on
012 200 7367 Or visit www.outofafrica.com.my

PINOTAGE
Beijing, China

Pinotage offers SABF-HK members 3 separate special benefits:

  1. As Pinotage is based outside the city centre a pick-up service or discount on travel fare to and from the restaurant is offered.
  2. Private dining facilities are available for those wishing to host business meetings whilst dining.
  3. Discounts are also offered on selected wines and meals, as well as certain South African drinks or desserts being offered complimentary.

For further details or bookings please contact Chris on 136112 07396 or 6430 7010 or email him at chrisb@winelink.com.cn

THE STOEP
Lantau, Hong Kong

The Stoep Restaurant serves South African cuisine and offers all members of the SABF-HK a 10% discount when joining them for a meal.

For further details or bookings please contact Mei Tai on 92341269 or 29802699.

MAKUMBA AFRICA LOUNGE BAR
Hong Kong

The Makumba Africa Lounge bar offers a regular special on Mondays and Tuesdays of 50% off their food and drinks menu.
*this offer does not apply to the snack menu

SABF-HK members are also offered a 20% discount from Wednesday-Saturday on their food and drinks bill, and are requested if possible to order in advance as Makumba Africa Lounge prefers to cook fresh on the day.

For further details or bookings please contact Amina Lamerre-Delafoulhouse on 852-2522 0544 or visit www.makumba.com.hk or http://singha.lonelyplanet.com/bars
